About Me

Hi—welcome to my little corner of the Internet, where I compose music. My name is Ben, and I go by BenGurWaves. I'm a 16-year-old self-funded artist who began this journey at the very start of 2025, pouring time, energy, and heart into every piece I create.

Music has always been part of me. I started playing piano at six, and over the years picked up guitar, violin, and more. I was always surrounded by melody—my childhood was full of sound, and I wrote my first original track, Crystal Waterfalls, at just nine years old.

This is a one-person operation: I write, arrange, and produce everything myself—just me, a piano, a computer, and whatever emotion flows through my fingers that day.

Big thanks to my cousin, who handles all the visuals and video editing. Together, we bring these songs to life across YouTube, Instagram, Rumble, Vimeo, Threads, and beyond.

If you'd like to support what I do, it truly means the world. But more than anything, I hope the music gives you a moment of stillness, inspiration, or clarity.

"All glory be to God. I am nothing without Him, and my music is nothing either. May God be praised through my music and through me."
