BenGurWaves: Artist Bio

Artist Bio

BenGurWaves, the musical project of 16-year-old Ben Gur, officially began in March 2025, marking the start of an ambitious journey into sound and storytelling. Born with an innate connection to music, Ben’s earliest compositions were experimental and raw, reflecting the natural growing pains of an artist finding his voice. By summer 2025, he had released ten tracks, each a testament to his dedication and rapidly developing skill. These initial releases captured the essence of his evolving sound: cinematic, emotive, and deeply personal.

In July 2025, after months of intensive work, Ben took a brief pause to reflect on his artistic direction. This moment of introspection proved transformative. Shifting his focus from merely crafting music to creating art, he began to approach composition as storytelling — each note, chord, and rhythm carrying emotion, narrative, and imagery. What had started as a hobby quickly evolved into a life’s pursuit.

Following this turning point, BenGurWaves released the album Six Views, a conceptual work exploring the subtle transitions of light and emotion. Each track corresponds to a different “view,” moving from the warm, golden tones of View I – Amber Veil to the dark, contemplative glow of View VI – Afterlight. The album weaves cinematic textures, piano-driven melodies, and atmospheric layers, offering listeners a journey through shifting landscapes of sound and feeling. Critics and listeners alike praised the album for its ability to evoke vivid imagery and emotional resonance, establishing BenGurWaves as a rising talent with a distinct artistic voice.

Looking ahead, BenGurWaves is preparing to release Finally Home in October 2025, an album that chronicles his personal growth and artistic evolution. The project blends intimate piano passages with layered instrumentation and ambient textures, reflecting themes of self-discovery, perseverance, and the search for belonging. This album is not just a collection of songs — it is a narrative, an immersive experience designed to connect listeners to the emotional core of Ben’s journey.

Beyond his albums, BenGurWaves’ approach to music emphasizes authenticity, experimentation, and storytelling. His compositions are crafted to resonate with both casual listeners and dedicated music enthusiasts, bridging the gap between technical mastery and emotional accessibility. Whether through solo piano pieces, cinematic arrangements, or complex soundscapes, Ben continues to explore the boundaries of his craft, always seeking to create art that speaks as much to the heart as to the ear.

BenGurWaves’ story is still unfolding, but his trajectory is clear: an artist whose commitment, vision, and talent have positioned him as a promising voice in contemporary instrumental music. From his earliest experiments to his most recent works, every note carries the promise of something more — a journey not just through sound, but through life itself.

Life Bio

Ben Gur was born in 2008 into a musical family. His great-grandmother, grandmother, and father were all musicians, weaving a generational legacy of sound. His father began his own journey in the Soviet Union, studying piano, guitar, and later violin. By his mid-twenties, he had composed four albums and, after immigrating to the United States, became deeply involved in local church music — leading choirs and singing groups.

Ben’s childhood followed a similar path. At six years old, he began piano lessons, quickly excelling beyond his peers. By eight, he was also studying saxophone in school band and performing with his church ensemble. At ten, he added guitar to his growing list of instruments, mastering it within three years. Alongside, he pursued the Royal Conservatory of Music piano program, completing six years’ worth of study in only three, and expanded into violin, cello, bass, and viola — much of it self-taught. By 15, Ben had amassed an impressive résumé: choirs, orchestras, solo performances, and proficiency across nearly every family of instruments.

Yet, for all his talent, Ben resisted creating his own music. Out of shyness, he declined encouragement from family, friends, and mentors. That changed after a pivotal trip to Missouri, where a heartfelt conversation with his cousin finally persuaded him to take the leap. In March 2025, BenGurWaves was born. His first attempts, however, lacked depth — uploads made out of habit rather than heart. But by July, after a short break, he realigned his vision. Music was not just a pastime; it was a calling.

That realization led to The Six Views Album, a glimpse of his artistic potential, followed by a vow he made to himself: every song would carry his full heart, or it would not exist at all. The result was Finally Home, released October 10, 2025 — an album born of honesty, perseverance, and the courage to begin again.

From generational legacy to personal breakthrough, Ben’s life is a story of sound — one that continues to grow louder, richer, and more profound with every passing year.
