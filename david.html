About David

I’m a kid who was always interested in filmmaking. I started dabbling in photography and filmmaking from a very young age, and over the years developed a passion for that art. Right now I am striving to write my first feature film, that will be meaningful and a direct representation of my faith.

On YouTube I’ve been blessed to work with some of my favorite brands in the film industry, like Zhiyun, Epidemic Sound, NANUK, FeiyuTech and many more.

I approach every project with intense pre-production, whether it is a small social media campaign or a short film. Organization, neat production, and clear communication with clients are key to my process. All made in my bedroom.

About David's Work

David is responsible for all the visual elements that complement BenGurWaves' music. His creative vision helps translate the emotional essence of each composition into visual form.

Through careful editing, thoughtful imagery, and atmospheric cinematography, David creates visual stories that enhance the listening experience and bring the music to life in a new dimension.

His work spans music videos, album art, promotional materials, and social media content — all crafted with the same attention to detail and emotional resonance that characterizes BenGurWaves' music.
